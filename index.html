About the Project

Edifica is a next-generation language learning application that blends artificial intelligence, gamification, and linguistic expertise to deliver effective, fun, and personalized education. Our app features three main modules: sentence pyramids, vocabulary with smart hints, and writing with role-play. Each module supports different language skills and is crafted with educational value in mind.

Modules

Language Pyramids

Build sentences step-by-step with AI-powered structure expansion.

Smart Vocabulary

Learn words with meaningful hints and spaced repetition techniques.

Role-Play Writing

Answer real-life questions and get feedback from AI. Great for creativity and empathy!

Email Module

Soon: Get motivational emails and reminders tailored to your learning goals.
